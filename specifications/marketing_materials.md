# War Cabinet: Marketing & Onboarding Strategy

This document outlines the strategic approach for persuading potential users pre-download, and ensuring high-retention onboarding post-download.

---

## 1. Pre-Download Persuasion (The Hook)
*Users need to realize immediately that this is not a basic grocery list spreadsheet, but a tactical logistics engine.*

### App Store / Play Store Presence
*   **The Trailer ("The Threat & The Solution")**: Do not just show screen-tapping. Frame the problem. 
    *   *Text overlay:* "When the supply chain breaks, you won't remember what's in your pantry." 
    *   *Visuals:* Show the Readiness Dashboard turning from Critical Red to Ready Green. Show the high-speed OCR scanner reading a blurry expiry date.
*   **Screenshot Callouts**: Focus on the *Intelligence*.
    *   "Thermal Guardrails prevent improper storage."
    *   "Pessimistic Preparedness Math exposes true logistical gaps."

### Social Media & Video Concepts (YouTube Shorts / TikTok)
Don't make "feature tutorials." Make "Scenario Resolutions."
*   **Video 1: The Logistics Audit**: "How to rotate a 6-month tactical food supply in under 30 seconds."
*   **Video 2: The Spreadsheet Flaw**: "Why most inventory apps fail (and how our pessimistic math prevents phantom surpluses)."
*   **Video 3: Field Operations**: "Scanning 20 items in 60 seconds with offline OCR extraction."

### The Landing Page / Developer Pitch (Hacker News)
*   **Philosophy over Features**: Preppers and tech enthusiasts love *logic*. Detail the internal engine: the Levenshtein distance brand deduplication, the offline-first SQLite event-sourcing, and the zero-trust mesh network logic.

## 2. In-App Onboarding (The Bootcamp)
*Do not overwhelm the user with 100 features on day one. Hook them with immediate value and progressive disclosure.*

### The Gamified "Rank" System
Leverage the existing `Missions` table to unlock features natively:
*   **Cadet Level (Day 1)**: Hide the complex global dashboards. The user's only mission: *Scan 1 Barcode and set an Expiry Date.*
*   **Corporal Level (Day 3)**: Introduce the Readiness Dashboard once they have enough items to actually generate a metric.

### The "Bootcamp" Freemium Model (Earned Scale vs. Instant Skip)
*Instead of a standard 7-day free trial, we use a gamified, data-capped onboarding system to drive product education and psychological investment.*

*   **The Bootcamp Limitations**: When a user downloads the app, they start as a Cadet. They are hard-capped on scale (e.g., maximum of 2 Cabinets, 3 Categories, 15 Items). To unlock more, they *must* complete specific feature-testing missions (e.g., "Log a deficit", "Catch an expired item", "Configure a tactical target").
*   **The Reward**: Once they complete the Bootcamp and reach the designated rank, they unlock **1 Free Month of "Field Marshall" (Premium)**, un-capping their data limits entirely. Because they *earned* it, they are highly invested and fully educated on the app's power.
*   **The "Instant Skip" (Monetization)**: For impatient power users who just want to import their 500-item pantry today, offer an "Honorable Discharge / Fast Track" button. They can skip the Bootcamp entirely by immediately subscribing to the "Private" tier, giving you an immediate monetization avenue for high-intent users.

### The "30-Day Preparedness Challenge"
*   In-app push notifications or prominent dashboard cards that guide new users in building a baseline survival cache.
*   *Week 1*: Secure 14 days of water and water purification tabs.
*   *Week 2*: Secure baseline carbohydrates (Rice/Pasta) and set tactical Min/Max targets using the Quick Target modal.

## 3. The Documentation (The Field Manual)
*   **Format**: A highly scannable, Notion/GitBook-style Wiki.
*   **Content Strategy**: Document the *why* alongside the *how*. 
    *   *Example Article*: "Understanding Pessimistic Readiness Metrics" (Explaining why a 200% surplus in baked beans shouldn't mask a 0% deficit in medical supplies).
    *   *Example Article*: "The Importance of Zero-Trust Mesh Sync" (Explaining the security of local WiFi syncing for household collaboration).
