# War Cabinet: Future Strategic Concepts

## Pinned Concepts
1. **NFC / QR Deep-Linking**
    *   **User Need**: Instant access to a specific physical shelf or cabinet.
    *   **Proposed Logic**: Small NFC stickers or QR labels placed on the physical cabinet door. Scanning them with the device instantly opens the app pre-filtered to that specific cabinet ID.
    *   **Status**: Parked.

2. **Automated Inventory Analysis**
    *   **User Need**: Predict when stock will run out based on consumption rates.
    *   **Status**: Draft.

3. **Multi-User Sync**
    *   **User Need**: Allow household members to contribute to the War Cabinet.
    *   **Status**: Draft.
