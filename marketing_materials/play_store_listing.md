# War Cabinet: Strategic Inventory Command 🛡️

**"Take Command of Your Logistics. Eliminate Expiry. Ensure Readiness."**

---

### 🛡️ THE MISSION
Is your pantry a logistics nightmare? Are you losing money to "Hidden Expiry" at the back of the shelf? **War Cabinet** was born from a singular vision: to provide a professional-grade command center for long-term stockpiling against **global supply chain disruptions** and **hyper-inflation**.

Designed for preppers, bulk-buyers, and efficiency-first households, War Cabinet gives you the "God's Eye View" of your entire supply chain—from the kitchen counter to the deep-storage cellar. No more "Inventory Drift," no more multi-buy waste—just absolute strategic readiness.

---

### ⚡ THE FRONT LINE (Direct Action)
No more digging through menus for your daily staples. The **Front Line Command Deck** puts your most-used items (Bread, Milk, staples) on a high-speed horizontal belt. 
*   **1-Tap Consumption:** Deduct stock with a single press.
*   **Intuitive Drift:** Items you use most naturally float to the premier leftmost position.
*   **Confirm & Capture:** Smart FEFO (First-Expiry, First-Out) logic automatically finds the soonest-expiring batch for you.

### 📡 UNIFIED COMMAND STRIP (Situational Awareness)
Deploy the **Tactical Command Strip** to sweep your entire inventory in milliseconds.
*   **High-Speed Search:** Instant character-by-character filtering across item names, categories, and locations.
*   **Multi-Site Sensors:** Filter by individual storage sites (Kitchen, Pantry, Cellar) or see a unified global audit.
*   **Urgency Brackets:** Quickly isolate critical items—`EXPIRED`, `THIS MONTH`, or `DUE SOON (<3M)`.

### 📉 THE WATERFALL of URGENCY
War Cabinet doesn't just list items; it **prioritizes threats**. Our recursive sorting engine ensures that the single most urgent batch in your entire stockpile is always the first thing you see.
*   **Tiered High-Contrast Signaling:** Colors only "flare up" when an item enters the critical 90-day rotation window.
*   **Smart Units:** Automatically scales weight and volume (g -> kg, ml -> l) for clean, professional data visualization.

### 🔔 STRATEGIC BRIEFINGS
Stay battle-ready without even opening the app.
*   **Smart Alerts:** Receive high-importance tactical briefings that flag aggregate quantities of items hitting the 1-month and 3-month windows.
*   **Deep-Link Directives:** Tap an alert to drop straight into a pre-filtered "Audit View" of exactly what needs rotating.

---

### ♟️ FEATURES AT A GLANCE:
*   **Multi-Cabinet Architecture:** Split your inventory by physical location.
*   **Smart Selection:** The app remembers your history and suggests batch sizes before you type.
*   **Tactical Totals:** Instant aggregates of kilos, liters, and units across all items.
*   **Offline First:** Encrypted local SQLite database ensures your data is yours—even when the grid goes dark.
