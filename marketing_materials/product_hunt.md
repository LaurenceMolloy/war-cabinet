## 2. The Maker Comment (The "Announcement")

**Headline: Why I built a "War Cabinet" for my kitchen.**

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I’m [Your Name], the creator of War Cabinet.

A few months ago, I found a bag of frozen peas at the back of my freezer. They were from 2021. They weren't food anymore; they were a archaeological artifact. 

It hit me then: the average household wastes 30% of their food simply because they *lose track of their own inventory*. At the same time, price rises and supply chain news have made many of us (myself included) want to keep a more resilient pantry. 

I looked for an app. Most were either boring spreadsheets or complicated retail inventory tools. I wanted something that felt **tactical**, **resilient**, and **intelligent**.

So, I built **War Cabinet**.

**What is it?**
It’s a stock management system designed with a "Logistics & Command" philosophy. It’s local-first (SQLite), privacy-focused, and built to help you stay one step ahead of waste and shortages.

### 🪖 Key Features:

*   **The Mess Hall (AI Intelligence):** Don't just list ingredients—deploy them. Our AI engine scans your current stock to suggest waste-conscious recipes based on what *must* be used now.
*   **Freezer Mode:** Standard apps use expiry dates. War Cabinet's Freezer Mode tracks **Age & Batch Rotation**, ensuring your deep storage stays fresh and rotated.
*   **The Quartermaster:** One-click low-stock reports and shareable "Deployment Lists" for your next supply run.
*   **Tactical Alerts:** Monthly "Deployment Briefings" that alert you to upcoming expiries and logistics gaps before they become problems.

### 🎖️ The Rank System:

We wanted to make the app accessible to everyone while funding its intelligence:
*   **Cadet (7-Day Evaluation):** Full access to everything. Try the AI, the alerts, and the freezer logistics.
*   **Private (Free Forever):** A generous capacity increase for civilian use. Perfect for small pantries.
*   **Sergeant (Professional Scale):** Unlimited logistics capacity and the full Quartermaster reporting suite.
*   **General (High-Command):** Subscription-based access to the AI "Mess Hall" and automated cloud-enhanced backups.

**My ask to you:**
I’d love your honest feedback on the "Tactical" UX. Does it make managing your household feel more like a mission and less like a chore? 

I’ll be here all day to answer your questions and take feature requests for the next "Deployment"!

Over and out. 🫡
