## 📜 THE LONG DEPLOYMENT (LONG DESCRIPTION)

**"Command Your Logistics. Eradicate Waste. Master Your Mess Hall."**

War Cabinet: The Smart Pantry. A professional grade kitchen inventory tool for pantry and freezer stocks, monitoring stock levels and expiry dates to eliminate waste using an AI Chef.

### 🛡️ THE MISSION

Is your pantry a logistics nightmare? Losing money to hidden food waste or rising costs? War Cabinet is your command centre for smarter food management - helping you stay organised, reduce waste, and stay ahead of supply disruptions.

Designed for preppers, bulk-buyers, and strategic households, War Cabinet gives you the "God's Eye View" of your entire supply chain - ensuring absolute operational readiness when the shelves go thin.


## THE MESS HALL (AI-POWERED RECIPES)

Don’t just track your food—put it to work. The Mess Hall turns your soon-to-expire ingredients into practical, great-tasting AI-generated meals.

Cook Like the Pros: Generate recipes inspired by famous chefs such as Gordon Ramsay, Jamie Oliver, and Nigella Lawson.

Custom Chef Memory: Not keen on the default names? Add any chef you like—War Cabinet remembers your last two for quick reuse.

Expiry-First Thinking: Recipes prioritise your soon-to-expire items while matching your tastes and dietary needs.

One-Tap Launch: Generate and send your personalised recipe prompt straight to ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude—ready to go.

### ⚡ THE FRONT LINE (DIRECT ACTION)
No more digging through menus for your daily staples. The **Front Line Command Deck** puts your most-used items on a high-speed horizontal belt. 
*   **1-Tap Consumption:** Deduct stock with a single press.
*   **Intuitive Drift:** Items you use most naturally float to the premier leftmost position.
*   **Smart Selection:** The app remembers your history and suggests batch sizes before you type.

### 📉 THE WATERFALL OF URGENCY (INVENTORY & EXPIRY TRACKER)
War Cabinet doesn't just list items; it **prioritizes threats**. Our recursive sorting engine ensures that the single most urgent batch in your entire stockpile is always the first thing you see.
*   **Tiered High-Contrast Signaling:** Visual alerts "flare up" when an item enters the critical 90-day rotation window.
*   **Smart Units:** Automatically scales weight and volume (g -> kg, ml -> l) for clean, professional data visualization.
*   **Multi-Site Sensors:** Filter by individual storage sites (Kitchen, Pantry, Cellar) or see a unified global audit.
