# User Journey: Stopping Food Waste Before It Happens

**The Question:** *"I'm planning meals for the week—what do we have in the cupboards that's going to expire soon so it doesn't end up in the bin?"*

We've all been there: finding a forgotten jar at the back of the cupboard that expired three months ago. It's frustrating and wasteful. This guide will show you how to use the app's visual Radar to instantly spot items that are nearing their use-by dates, so you can easily plan them into your meals and avoid throwing good food away.

---

### Step 1: Get the Bird's Eye View
When you open the **Radar View**, you're looking at your entire home's inventory at a glance. The inner ring shows your main categories (like 'Pantry' or 'Cleaning'), and the outer ring breaks those down into specific products.

In the very centre, you'll see your quick-glance expiry blocks:
- **Red:** Expired or expiring right now
- **Orange:** Expiring in the next 1–3 months
- **Yellow:** Expiring in 4–6 months

If you see numbers in the Red or Orange boxes, it's time to investigate!

---

### Step 2: Zooming In on the Problem
Let's find those expiring items. 

1. Look at the outer rings of the Radar. You might notice some segments have thin coloured lines or markers within them—these correspond to the expiry colours. 
2. Tap on the category segment that looks like it holds the expiring items. 
3. **Pro Tip:** If you have a lot of items, those outer segments can be quite small and hard to tap accurately with a finger. Use the **on-screen joystick** at the bottom! Tapping the arrows lets you cleanly step left or right through the segments without any mis-taps.

> *[Suggested Image: A screenshot showing the joystick controls being highlighted, perhaps with a finger icon pressing the 'right' arrow.]*

---

### Step 3: Following the Trail
As you select a category, the Radar zooms in to show you the products within it. 
- Take a look at the selected segment. You'll notice it now has a crisp **white border** around it so you always know exactly where you are.
- At the top left and right of the screen, handy counters will appear, letting you know exactly how many products and batches live inside this category.

Keep drilling down by selecting the specific product, and finally, the batch. 

> *[Suggested Image: A screenshot midway through a drill-down (Product level), clearly showing the white highlight border around the active segment and the new counters in the top corners.]*

---

### Step 4: The Final Details
Once you reach the **Batch Level**, the radar will have zoomed all the way in. The centre of the circle will now update to show you the exact details of what you've selected. 

Here, you'll see the specific brand, the quantity, and most importantly, the exact **Expiry Date** highlighted clearly for you. 

**Pro Tip:** If the Radar segments look a bit tight, **tap the black centre hub** at any time. This will physically magnify the entire visualization, making it much easier to read product names and spot those tiny expiry markers!

Now you know exactly which items to pull to the front of the cupboard and use for dinner tonight!

> *[Suggested Image: A screenshot of the final Batch-level view, with the central hub displaying the specific item details and its expiry date.]*

---

### Step 5: The Cabinet Pivot (Power User Shortcut)
Finding that expiring tin of beans in the Garage Freezer is great, but while you're out there grabbing it, shouldn't you check what else needs using?

This is where the Radar gets really smart. When you are zoomed all the way in on an item, you'll see its physical location (like "GARAGE FREEZER") displayed right at the bottom of the central hub.
- **Tap that location!** 
- The Radar will instantly filter itself to *only* show you the contents of that specific cabinet, without losing your place on the beans you were just looking at.
- You can now spin around and see exactly what else is expiring in the Garage Freezer.
- Once you're done, simply tap the **ZOOM OUT TO BUNKER VIEW** button in the centre to instantly zoom back out and see your entire home again. 

It's the absolute fastest way to seamlessly jump between hunting down a specific ingredient and managing an entire cupboard!
