# User Journey: Strategic Resupply & Logistics

**The Question:** *"We're doing a big shop this weekend—how do I know what we're actually low on across the whole house without checking every single cupboard?"*

Managing a household's essentials can feel like a part-time job. Between the pantry, the garage freezer, and the cleaning cupboard, it's easy to lose track of what's running low. This guide will show you how to use the **Logistics Command Centre** to instantly generate a master resupply list based on your own custom stock thresholds.

---

### Step 1: Accessing the Quartermaster
Tap the **Logistics** tab at the bottom of your screen. You're now in the "Quartermaster" view—the app's brain for supply chain and readiness.

You'll see three main modes at the top:
- **Readiness:** A high-level health check of your inventory.
- **Resupply:** Your "Shopping List" view, showing everything below your set minimums.
- **Rotation:** A tactical view for moving older stock to where it will be used first.

Tap **RESUPPLY** to see your active shortfalls.

---

### Step 2: Understanding the Intelligence
The Resupply list is grouped by category (e.g., 'PROTEINS', 'STARCHES'). For every item shown, the app performs a real-time calculation:

- **Current Stock:** The total quantity you have stored anywhere in the house.
- **The Deficit (MIN):** Highlighted in **Red**. This is how much you need to buy *immediately* just to reach your baseline safety level.
- **The Target (MAX):** Highlighted in **Blue**. This shows how much you'd need to buy to fully replenish your stock to its maximum capacity.

If you've set a "Pack Size" for an item (like "500g Pasta"), the app will even suggest exactly how many packs to buy (e.g., "3 x 500g").

---

### Step 3: Sharing the Manifest
Once you've reviewed the list, you don't need to write anything down. 
1. Tap the **SHARE** button in the top right corner.
2. The app will generate a clean, text-based **Bunker Resupply Report**.
3. You can instantly email this to yourself, a partner, or share it directly to your favourite notes or messaging app.

It’s a professional-grade shopping list generated in two taps.

> *[Suggested Image: A screenshot of the system share sheet open over the Resupply view, showing the "War Cabinet Restocking Report" text preview.]*

---

### Step 4: Maintaining Readiness (Pro Tip)
To make this view useful, you need to tell the app what "Low" looks like for you.
1. Go to the **Catalog** tab.
2. Select an Item Type (e.g., 'Rice').
3. Set your **Min Stock Level** (the point where the alarm should trigger) and **Max Stock Level** (your ideal full pantry).

Once these are set, the Quartermaster handles the rest—calculating your deficits across every cupboard and freezer in the house.
